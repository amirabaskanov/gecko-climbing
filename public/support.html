Frequently asked questions

How do I log a climbing session?

Tap the center "+" tab to start a new session. Add climbs with their grade and outcome (flash, send, project, or attempt), then save when you're done.

What do the outcomes mean?

Flash = completed on the first attempt. Send = completed in 2+ attempts. Project = still working on it. Attempt = tried but didn't complete.

Can I delete a session?

Yes. Open the session from your Sessions tab, then swipe left or use the delete option.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and search for climbers by their display name. Tap Follow to see their sessions in your feed.

How do I turn off notifications?

Open Profile → Settings → Notifications. You can toggle weekly recaps, dormant reminders, and friend activity independently.
